# Accessibility Rules

Defines accessibility standards for MedLens.

---

## 1. Core Principles

### 1.1 Inclusive Design
App must be usable by:
- low literacy users
- non-native English users

---

### 1.2 Readability First
Content must be:
- simple
- clear
- structured

---

## 2. Text Accessibility

- use plain language
- avoid jargon
- support ELI12 mode

---

## 3. Visual Accessibility

- high contrast text
- readable font sizes

---

## 4. Interaction Accessibility

- large tap targets
- clear feedback

---

## 5. Assistive Support

- screen reader compatibility
- clear labels

---

## 6. Summary

Accessibility ensures:
- usability for all users
- clarity of medical information
